Our Legacy of Luxury

Founded in 2008 by visionary architect Marcus Binge, our resort was conceived as a sanctuary where innovation meets tradition. What began as a single architectural marvel—the 40-foot raised dining platform—has evolved into South Africa's most exclusive luxury destination.

Every aspect of Binge Leisure Resort is meticulously designed to offer unparalleled value. Our commitment extends beyond luxury to sustainable excellence, with eco-friendly practices integrated into every operation. The resort's architecture harmonizes with the surrounding landscape, creating a seamless blend of natural beauty and human ingenuity.

Today, we host discerning travelers from across the globe, each seeking the unique experience that only elevation—both physical and experiential—can provide. Our team of 150 dedicated professionals ensures every moment exceeds expectations.

Marcus Binge
