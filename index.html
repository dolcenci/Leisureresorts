Dining Above the Clouds

Our signature raised dining area stands 40 feet above ground, offering an unparalleled culinary experience. Accessed via an elegant staircase, guests are treated to gourmet meals while enjoying breathtaking views of the resort and natural dam.

The structure is supported by reinforced poles and designed by award-winning architects to provide both safety and aesthetic appeal. This unique dining experience has become the hallmark of Binge Leisure Resort.
